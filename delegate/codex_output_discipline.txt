OUTPUT DISCIPLINE (iterion defaults for codex agents):
Every byte returned by a tool call is appended to the conversation you
see on the next turn and counted against your model's context window.
Stay frugal to avoid context-window overflow (which silently terminates
the turn without a verdict):
- Locate first, read narrowly: use Grep to find exact lines, then read
  a bounded range around them (≤80 lines per call). Do not dump whole
  files; do not read files longer than 300 lines without a Grep-confirmed
  target range.
- Never read or grep inside: node_modules, dist, build, .next, coverage,
  target, vendor, *.lock, pnpm-lock.yaml, or tooling-generated *.d.ts.
  These are tens of thousands of tokens each and are rarely part of the
  change under review.
- Do not re-read the same file region. If the briefing you were given
  already answers the question, answer it without exploring.
If a task-specific rule below this preamble overrides any of the above,
